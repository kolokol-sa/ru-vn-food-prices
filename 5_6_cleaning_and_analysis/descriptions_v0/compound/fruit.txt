**Fruit**  

- Significantly Cheaper in Vietnam:  
    - Mango (3.2x cheaper).  
    - Banana (38% cheaper overall, or 25% for long bananas).
    - Oranges (28% cheaper overall, but imported oranges in Russia are more affordable than imported oranges in Vietnam).  

- Key Observations:  
    - Fruit prices in Vietnam are generally lower, likely due to local production and lower transportation costs.
