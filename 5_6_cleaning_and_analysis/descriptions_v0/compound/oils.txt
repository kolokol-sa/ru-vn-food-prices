**Cooking oils**
- Prices:
    - Russia:
        - Sunflower Oil: Median = 1.76 USD/l (~159 RUB/l; ~44800 VND/l).
    - Vietnam:
        - Sunflower Oil: Median = 3.24 USD/l (~293 RUB/l; ~82500 VND/l).  
        - Soybean Oil: Median = 2.54 USD/l (~230 RUB/l; ~64900 VND/l).
- Affordability:
    - In Russia, sunflower oil is a staple and significantly cheaper than oils in Vietnam.  
    - In Vietnam, cooking oils are generally more expensive, with sunflower oil priced considerably higher than in Russia.
