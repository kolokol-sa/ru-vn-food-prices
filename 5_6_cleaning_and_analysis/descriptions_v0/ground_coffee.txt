- Key Observations:
    - Ground coffee is significantly cheaper in Vietnam, likely due to local production, while in Russia it’s mostly imported.
    - In Russia there are affordable coffees with prices comparable to Vietnam's median, but more than 90% of items are still significantly more expensive.
    - Russia’s larger variety may contribute to the higher median price.
